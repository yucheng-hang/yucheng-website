THE MOBILE PHONE ERA


Overview

The Mobile Phone Era is a series of infographics that reveal the mobile phone usage of different age groups. It horizontally compares the frequency, the time slots, and the time spent on different activities of mobile phones between the three age groups.

Mobile phones have become such powerful and versatile tools. We use them for every aspect of our lives: work, school, entertainment and relationships. However, we rarely think about what proportion of our lives has mobile phones occupied. I hope this project can let us better understand how time is wasted on our mobile phones, so as to arouse our reflection on mobile phone addiction.
