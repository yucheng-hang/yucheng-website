Biography

Yucheng Hang is a UX/UI designer, creative technologist and multimedia artist. Born in Yangzhou, China, she is currently based in Storrs, Connecticut. She accomplished her BE in Digital Media Technology from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2019. During that time, Yucheng grew interests in the intersection of design and technology. She started to transform emerging technologies to digital experiences resonate with emotion and warmth. With that motivation in mind, she developed creative projects in the area of interaction design, web design and data visualization.

Artist Statement

I always insist that the duty of a designer is to solve problems. Designers are responsible to see through all the fog and clutter and create solutions that propel people forward. Empathy and storytelling are two methods I use to access the goal. Capturing what users see, feel and experience by research methods and empathy, I try my best to understand users' expressions, needs, motivations and limitations. Good stories captivate audiences and convey values in an immersive way. I utilize storytelling to create pleasant experiences that foster better relationships between humans, products and society.
