E-SCREEN


Overview

E-Screen is a digital screen that is applicable for various styles of airport trolleys. It can realize online check-in, flight query and indoor navigation in airports.

Almost all products on the market that provide flight query and navigation functionalities are in the form of App.However, when people use the airport trolley to carry their baggage, both their hands are occupied, therefore, it becomes very inconvenient for them to use their phones. E-screen is designed to add on the airport trolley. Passengers can operate the screen with buttons on the handles so that they can navigate or inquiry while they push the trolley.
